Data Science & Analysis

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed to solve puzzles. So that’s how I look at big data sets: to me it is one big puzzle I want to solve. Finding patterns nobody else sees is the challenge to me. Getting insights out of data is something that I really like. Data visualization and machine learning are some techniques that I am using to achieve this.

I recently got a scholarship at shift academy boot camp in the data science field. I have completed the University of Michigan Python for Everybody Specialties too.
